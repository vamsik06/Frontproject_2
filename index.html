Albert Einstein

The man who revolutionized physics

Here's a timeline of Einstein's life:

1879

Born in Ulm, Germany to Hermann and Pauline Einstein. His family moved to Munich when he was one year old.

1895

At age 16, Einstein wrote his first scientific paper "The Investigation of the State of Aether in Magnetic Fields"

1900

Graduated from the Swiss Federal Polytechnic in Zurich with a degree in physics and mathematics

1905

Published four groundbreaking papers including the Special Theory of Relativity and the famous equation E=mcÂ²

1915

Completed the General Theory of Relativity, which revolutionized our understanding of gravity

1919

The theory of relativity was proven correct during a solar eclipse, making Einstein world-famous

1921

Awarded the Nobel Prize in Physics for his explanation of the photoelectric effect

1933

Emigrated to the United States to escape Nazi persecution, becoming a professor at Princeton University

1939

Wrote a letter to President Roosevelt warning about the potential development of atomic weapons

1952

Offered the presidency of Israel (declined), stating "I am deeply moved by the offer from our State of Israel, and at once saddened and ashamed that I cannot accept it."

1955

Died in Princeton, New Jersey at the age of 76. His last words were in German to a nurse who didn't understand the language.
